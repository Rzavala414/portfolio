Explor

Explor was created with one goal in mind, to reduce the stress of travelers when preparing for a big trip. With Explor you can relax just a bit more and let us do some of the heavy lifting. Simply input your destination along with travel dates, and once we receive your information we compile a list of top restaurants, popular attractions, weather, and a tailored check list for your vacation. In the future we plan on expanding our application by adding the ability to choose flights and housing.
